# La paresse de penser — companion code & resources

Companion repository for ***La paresse de penser — Pourquoi nous laissons l'IA décider à notre place*** by [Mastafa Foufa](https://mastafafoufa.com).

**FYP éditions · 22 May 2026 · 🇫🇷 French · Preface by Luc Julia, co-creator of Siri.**

> *If AI is doing the heavy lifting, who's doing the thinking?*

---

## What's in this repo

- **Notebooks** — experiments and demos referenced in the book.
- **Exercises** — prompts and questions to practice critical thinking *with* AI tools, not despite them.
- **Further reading** — papers, articles, and tools cited in the book.

🚧 Active — content lands as we approach launch and in the weeks following. Star ⭐ to follow along.
